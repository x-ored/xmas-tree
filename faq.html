We offer free ground shipping for all orders in the contiguous Toronto and GTA area. Other orders will be charged a shipping fee. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return or exchange the product within 30 days of receipt or prior to December 25 (whichever comes first).

Shipping timelines depend on the shipping method you choose. You should receive a shipping confirmation email within 24 hours of placing your order. Standard shipping typically takes 2-5 business days to arrive.

Cancellation requests may be submitted within 30 minutes of placing an order. After this window, the order will be forwarded to the warehouse for processing and shipping and can no longer be altered. You may still return or exchange your merchandise in accordance with our returns and exchange policies. Refusing to accept delivery of any merchandise will result in a $75 service fee (per box).

Return or exchange eligible non-perishable products within 30 days of receipt or before December 25 (whichever comes first). Please use our online customer service forms to initiate a return or exchange. Return (Non-perishable): Once your item has been shipped back to us, we will process your refund less any applicable shipping fees. Exchange: Exchanges for items of lesser value will be assessed a small exchange shipping fee. We waive this fee for exchanges for items of equal or greater value. Christmas Garden reserves the right to reject items that are ineligible for return, and to assess an additional return fee. Products ineligible for return include perishable items, personalized items, items that are damaged due to improper care or use, and items for which a return has not been authorized. A $75 per box service fee will be charged for all delivery refusals.

When placing an order through our Internet Ordering system you can choose to pay with Cash, Credit or Debit. All of these payment methods can be made at the door (if delivery) and online (credit and debit only). When paying with Credit or Debit for the delivery option, our drivers will be happy to assist you with Credit/Debit machines.

All items are packed in branded packaging. A stand is always included with the Christmas tree.
